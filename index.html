Turn: White
Help: 'H' Key

Controls & Rules:

Mouse Drag and Drop Select a piece to move and drop it on a square

P: Toggle on or off the point light

M: Toggle on or off the shadows

H: Show this menu

Rules: Game begins with White moving first. Pieces are always moved diagonally. Regular pieces can only move forward while Kings can move forward and backwards. Kings are created when a players piece reached the last row of the board. You can capture opposing pieces by "jumping" the piece and you can do multiple "jumps" in one turn. The game ends when one player captures all of the other players pieces.
